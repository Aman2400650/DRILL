Fire Saftey & Preparedness

*Saftey Guidelines

1. Drop, Cover, And Hold On During Tremors.


2. Stay Away From Windows And Heavy Objects.


3. Keep An Emergency Kit Ready.


4. Move To An Open Space After The Shaking Stops.


*SUMMARY


1. Prepare Before an Fire Hazard.

  • Check all wiring for damage or loose connections.
  • Make sure LPG regulator and cylinder are in good condition.
  • Check for gas leaks using soap-water test (never with a matchstick)
  • Train students and staff to use fire extinguishers (PASS method).


2. What to Do During an Fire Hazard.

  • Raise an Immediate Alarm by Shouting “Fire! Fire!” or Activate the fire alarm or Inform teachers, wardens, and emergency authorities..
  • Do NOT use elevators/lifts — use stairs only.
  • Move in a line without running or pushing.
  • Follow the marked emergency exit route.
  • Do NOT use water on electrical equipment.
  • Follow the NDMA PASS Method:
  • P – Pull the pin
  • A – Aim at the base of the fire
  • S – Squeeze the handle
  • S – Sweep side to side


3. After the Fire Hazard.

  • Do NOT enter the building until firefighters or authorities declare it safe.
  • Provide first aid until medical help arrives or Call 108 For Medical Emergencies./li>
  • Open windows and doors to remove smoke.
  • Use phones only for emergencies to keep lines open.
  • Notify the Fire Department, NDMA officials (if required), and campus/security
